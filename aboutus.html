Welcome To Aga Khan University Hospital

About Us

Aga Khan University Hospital is a private, not-for-profit hospital providing quality health care services to all those who are in need. The Hospital has set the standard for comprehensive healthcare and modern medical education in East Africa. Our dedicated staff, advanced facilities and state-of-the-art technologies have earned the hospital great reputation as a leading medical institution and teaching hospital in the region, and beyond

Our Mission

To provide healthcare to those in the region and beyond thus promoting mental and physical wellness within the community

Our Vision

To be a leading quality healthcare provider globally and offer world-class services.
